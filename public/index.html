Curriculum

What You Will Learn

Six focused modules covering the full spectrum of stock market trading — from reading your first chart to executing live trades.

Technical Analysis

Understand price action, support and resistance levels, trend identification, and how to use indicators to time your entries and exits.

Chart Reading

Read candlestick patterns, chart formations, and volume signals. Know what the market is telling you before you place a single trade.

Risk Management

Learn position sizing, stop-loss placement, and how to protect your capital. The difference between traders who last and those who don't.

Portfolio Building

Structure a balanced portfolio, diversify across sectors, and build a strategy that grows your wealth over time without unnecessary exposure.

Market Psychology

Control fear and greed. Understand crowd behavior, market cycles, and how to stay disciplined when the market tests your conviction.

Live Trade Execution

Watch real trades placed in real time. See how analysis translates into action — entries, exits, and trade management explained step by step.
